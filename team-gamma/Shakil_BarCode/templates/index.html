Frequently Asked Questions

What barcode formats are supported for generation?

Currently, we support generating Code 128, EAN-13, and Code 39 barcodes. These cover most retail and inventory needs.

Do you store my scanned barcodes?

No. We process the images temporarily to extract the barcode data and immediately delete them from our servers.

Why is my camera not working?

Ensure you have granted camera permissions in your browser. Live scanning works best with autofocus cameras.

Is this service free to use?

Yes, ScanFusion is completely free with no registration or login required.
